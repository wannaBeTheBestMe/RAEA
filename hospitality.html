Our Amazing Service Features

Facilities available in for conferences, Seminars, meetings and training programmes

Conference Hall

A well-equipped aesthetically designed and decorated airconditioned conference hall can accommodate 100 people. Latest audio-visual systems, projector and screen are well fitted in the conference hall. Computer and sound recording systems are available in the conference hall. Also, there are two attached committee rooms to the conference hall.



Shakahar

A Spacious Place for Community Eating

The big dining hall is on the first floor with an open-air terrace surrounded by lush green Jamun trees. Shakahar provides fresh vegetarian food with special Rajasthani recepies. Around 100 people can relish food in Shakahar's peaceful environment.



Arrangement for Stay

There are two types of stay accommodations available on the campus:
(i)Three double-bed guest rooms with attached toilets.
(ii)Three dormitories, which can accommodate 40 participants.



A Rich Library and a Reading Hall

In the basement of building, there is a spacious, airy and lighted library with a good collection of books and journals. 120 young people, students, research scholars use this peaceful learning space with a collection of around eleven thousand pricious books around.
