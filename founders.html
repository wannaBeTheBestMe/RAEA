Anil Bordia

Mr Anil Bordia, the former Secretary of Education, Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India who also served as the Director Primary and Secondary Education in Rajasthan; was the founder of this organisation. He is no more with us but his guiding and inspiring spirit walks with us everday. He is always being fondly remembered. We organise Shri Anil Bordia Memorial Lecture ever year. The First Memorial Lecture was delivered by Smt Aruna Roy, Second Memorial Lecture was delivered by Dr Gopal Krishan Gandhi, former Governer of West Bengal, Third was delivered by Dr Krishan Kumar, former Director of NCERT and educationist of his own eminence. Recently the Fourth Anil Bordia Memorial Lecture was delivered by Shri Sharad Chandra Behar, former Chief Secretery of MP. Recently the fifth was delivered by Mr Madhav Chavam CEO, Pradham.

Vishnu Dutt Sharma

Justice BP Beri was the Founder President of RAEA, Shri Lalit Kishore Lohmi the eminent educationist of Rajasthan was the Founder Secretary of RAEA. Shri Vishnu Dutt Sharma Former Home Secretary of Govt. of Rajasthan, joined RAEA as Second President after Justice Beri. Miss Krishna Terve Principal of Kanodia Collage and Shri Kalyan Jaisani of Rajasthan Vidhyapeeth, Udaipur, Shri R.S. Kumat and Shri Tej Karan Dandia were also associated as our founder office bearers. Other stalwarts and dignitaries associated with RAEA were Dr. Mohan Singh Mehta Former High Commissioner of India in Pakistan and Founder Vice Chancellor of University of Rajasthan, Dr. Jagan Nath Singh Mehta, Shri Sadashiv Ram Sharma, Shri S.N. Rao, Shri Ajit Kumar Jain, Bhai Bhawan, Shri Shubhu Patva, Dr. Chhagan Mohta from Bikaner, the Humanist Philosopher and an eminent interprettor of various philosophical texts and also a critic of contemporary creative literary works Shri M L Mehta, Smt. Kanta Marwah, Dr. M.K. Marwah, Shri Rajendra Bhanawat, Shri Om Srivastav, Shri Sushil Kumar Dashora, Shri Kishore Saint and Shri Nand Kishore Acharya were also part of this organisation. With the blessings of these luminaries the institution had a humble, simple but majestic take off in 1973.
