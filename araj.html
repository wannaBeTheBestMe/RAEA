About Rajasthan

Rajasthan is a north western state of India. Western side of the state shares land border with pakistan. It is also called Thar Desert. This area is covered by huge shifting sand dunes. The state shares borders with Punjab, Haryana, Delhi, Gujarat and Madhya Pradesh.
